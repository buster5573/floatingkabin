This is a virtual entity owned by Alex To. What kind of creation and why is it named "Floating Kabin"?

Alex loves to express what exists in his/her creative world. Sometimes it's a drawing, sometimes it's an essay. As his/her creation piled up, Alex wanted to build a blog from the scratch. Alex thought that's way cooler than using one of those major platforms.

What does Floating Kabin mean? One day, Alex met a fortune teller, who described Alex's life as a bottle on the ocean. With his/her fascination towards Japanese culture, Alex named this entity as "遊泳花瓶", or "Floating Kabin"
